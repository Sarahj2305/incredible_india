Nov 29

Wedding Exhibition at Leela Palace
Time: 10:00 am-10:00pm

We are happy to announce the 10th Edition of the most prestigious exhibition, "Times Asia Wedding Fair 2016", Udaipur.. India's Largest Wedding and Exhibition with over 300 booths from all over India and abroad.

Parineeti will be held only for one day in Udaipur, India. This show will provide an ideal platform to the interested bride and groom and their family members as well as eminent experts related to wedding industry to share their business knowledge and expertise with each other. At the same time the main objective of this expo is to draw the attention of the wedding couples by bringing forward different types of innovative wedding preparations products, services and techniques. Parineeti will include some of the highlights like networking opportunities and services, launches of various kinds of wedding and bridal related products and accessories and one to one meeting with the interested buyers. The wedding couples can gather useful information by attending this show.
