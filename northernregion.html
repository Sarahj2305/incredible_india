WHEN IN THE NORTH

Take a journey into the rugged foothills of the Himalayas, starting with the spectacular Golden Temple in Amritsar in the Punjab. Embark on a panoramic train journey or trek within breathtaking vistas of snow-capped scenery. Experience all the stories narrated by Rudyard Kipling in the backdrop of Dehradun. Explore the hills and remote. Buddhist monasteries at the high-altitude hill stations of Shimla where Kipling wrote Kim and Dharamsala home of the Dalai Lama.
